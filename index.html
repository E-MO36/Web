What is I Love Boobies?

I Love Boobies ($Boobies) is more than just a meme coin; it's a movement built on compassion and community. We leverage the power of cryptocurrency to raise funds for individuals battling breast cancer, directly contributing to GoFundMe campaigns to cover crucial treatment costs. Our transparent approach ensures every donation makes a tangible impact.

Our Mission

Community-Driven

We believe in the power of a united community to create positive change. Our token holders are the backbone of our mission.

Direct Impact

Funds raised are sent directly to verified GoFundMe pages, ensuring they reach those in need without delay.

Transparency

All transactions and donations are publicly viewable on the blockchain, providing full transparency to our supporters.

How We Donate

Creator funds collected from Pump.fun are directly converted into donations, which we then contribute to the selected GoFundMe campaigns. This ensures that a portion of every transaction supports our charitable mission.
